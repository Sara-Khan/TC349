This is going to be a short post, because it's pretty obvious what I'm talking about here. Chocolate chips + Pancakes = Win. I mean, who doesn't love chocolate chips? (Unless you're allergic...) Throw them in pancakes and you have the best invention ever! Gimme a "Like" if you agree! ;-)

Buckwheat Pancakes = FAIL

Now I'm not trying to hate on people who have to adhere to certain dietary restrictions, but if you DON'T know what a pancake tastes like then DON'T EVER try these things. They're a mockery to all things pancake. If you HAVE had pancakes, and have despairingly lost the ability to enjoy them, don't substitue for these instead. Try something that is a little closer to their texture and succulence; like cardboard...
